## What Is spot.ai?

spot.ai is a **video intelligence platform** that combines purpose-built hardware (the Intelligent Video Recorder), AI-powered software, and a modern cloud dashboard to turn your cameras into active business tools.

The platform is built on a **hybrid cloud architecture** — meaning the AI processing happens locally at the edge for speed and reliability, while the cloud provides remote access, search, and management from anywhere.

## The spot.ai Platform

### AI Cameras
spot.ai is fully **camera-agnostic** — it works with any ONVIF-compliant IP camera through the Intelligent Video Recorder. Already have cameras? They instantly gain AI capabilities when connected to the platform. Need new cameras? spot.ai provides **premium IP cameras at no additional cost** with your subscription.

### Intelligent Video Recorder (IVR)
The brain of the system. The IVR is a purpose-built appliance that:
- **Auto-discovers** cameras on your network
- **Encrypts** and stores footage locally (30, 60, or 90-day retention)
- **Runs deep-learning AI models** at the edge — no cloud dependency for real-time detection
- Supports **up to 32 cameras** per appliance, with seamless multi-appliance scaling

### Video Intelligence
This is where spot.ai truly shines. The AI indexes every frame of video with deep-learning models, enabling:
- **Natural language search** — Search footage like you search the web. "Person at back door after 10pm" returns instant results.
- **Object detection** — Automatically classifies people, vehicles, forklifts, and other assets
- **People counting** — Track foot traffic, occupancy, and peak hours over time
- **License plate recognition (LPR)** — Read and search plates across all cameras
- **Trend reporting** — AI-generated reports on traffic patterns, incidents, and operational metrics

### Real-Time Alerts
Stop watching cameras and start responding to events:
- **Instant notifications** via email, SMS, or mobile app — with video clips attached
- **Custom triggers** — People in restricted zones, vehicles idle too long, PPE violations, or any custom scenario
- **Safety monitoring** — Automatically detect safety violations and hazardous conditions
- **Alert analytics** — Track frequency, response times, and trends over time
