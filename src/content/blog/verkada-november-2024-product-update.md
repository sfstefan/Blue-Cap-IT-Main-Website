Verkada's November 2024 product update brings significant improvements across the entire platform — from brand-new hardware to powerful AI search features and expanded mobile capabilities. Here's a full breakdown of everything in this release.

## Cameras: New Hardware & AI-Powered Search

### CD63 & CD63-E Dome Cameras

Verkada introduces the **CD63 (indoor) and CD63-E (outdoor) dome cameras**, featuring superior image quality powered by a new AI-capable processor. These models deliver sharper video, better analytics performance, and a streamlined installation process that makes deployment faster and easier.

### AI-Powered Reverse Image Search

One of the most exciting additions this release is **AI-powered reverse image search**. You can now upload a photo and Verkada will find matching persons, vehicles, or objects across your entire camera fleet using advanced pixel and pattern analysis. This is a major time-saver for investigations — instead of manually scrubbing through hours of footage, simply upload a reference image and let the AI do the work.

### Occupancy Trends Enhancements

**Occupancy trends** get a meaningful upgrade with multiple count-line presets, the ability to track both people and vehicles with averaging, and new in/out graphing. These improvements provide richer operational insights for retail locations, offices, lobbies, and other high-traffic environments.

### Quick Alert Creation

Verkada now lets you **convert any search query into an alert with a single click** directly from the search interface. This streamlines the process of setting up monitoring rules — find what matters, then immediately start getting notified about it.

### Command Connector Updates

The **Command Connector** expands its capabilities with custom NTP/NTS support, new EU and Australia data center options, and compatibility with **over 1,000 non-Verkada cameras**. This makes it easier than ever to bring third-party camera systems under Verkada's unified management platform.

### Granular API & Webhook Permissions

For organizations with more advanced integration needs, Verkada now offers **customizable API keys with product-line-specific access** and selective event forwarding via webhooks. This means you can create API credentials that are scoped to exactly what each integration needs — nothing more, nothing less.

### New Dome Mount (ACC-MNT-REC-2)

A new **flush ceiling mount** is now available for dome cameras, supporting ceilings up to 50mm thick. This provides a clean, professional installation look for indoor deployments.

### CM41-S End-of-Sale Notice

The **CM41-S camera** will be discontinued on February 1, 2025. Verkada recommends the **CM42-S** as the replacement model. If you're currently using or planning to order CM41-S units, reach out to us to discuss your transition options.

## Alarms: Completely Redesigned

### New Verkada Alarms

Verkada has **completely redesigned its alarm system** from the ground up. The new alarms deliver:

- **Flexible arming and disarming** options for different operational scenarios
- **Reduced false alarms** through smarter detection logic
- **Informed response capabilities** that give responders better context before they arrive on scene
- **Cost-effective legacy system replacement** — making it easier to retire outdated alarm panels

### Classic Alarms End-of-Sale

Legacy alarm SKUs will be **discontinued on February 7, 2025**. Existing deployments will continue to receive support, but new purchases should use the redesigned alarm system going forward.

## Access Control: New Roles & Automated Credential Management

### Updated Management Roles

Access control management gets a clearer structure with a **three-category role system** covering system roles, user roles, and site roles. This makes it easier to assign the right permissions to the right people across your organization.

### Cross-Site Access Levels

You can now **deploy credentials that span multiple sites**, eliminating the need to configure access separately at each location. This is a major efficiency gain for multi-site organizations like school districts, retail chains, and corporate campuses.

### SCIM-Synced Credentials

Verkada now integrates with **Okta and Microsoft Entra ID** to automatically link physical access credentials to user identities. When someone is provisioned or deprovisioned in your identity provider, their physical access credentials update automatically — no manual intervention required.

### User Profile Photo APIs

New **profile photo APIs** let you automate the syncing of employee photos from your identity management system directly into Verkada. This keeps badge photos current and reduces administrative overhead.

## Command Platform: Mobile Expansion & Smarter Navigation

### Mobile App — Intercom & Sensor Support

The **Command mobile app** continues to close the gap with the desktop experience. You can now receive intercom calls, unlock doors, and view live footage directly from your phone — bringing intercom and sensor functionality to the mobile platform.

### Alert Inbox Navigation

The alert inbox gets a usability upgrade with **custom sections, a view-all option, and starred alert functionality**. These improvements make it easier to organize, prioritize, and revisit important alerts without losing track of anything.

### OIDC Protocol Support

Verkada adds **OpenID Connect (OIDC) protocol support** for secure identity authentication across both web and mobile platforms. This is an important addition for organizations with strict identity and access management requirements.

## Connectivity: Carrier Flexibility & Global Redundancy

### US Carrier Selection

Organizations in the United States can now **choose their primary cellular carrier** for Verkada SIMs on a per-site basis. This gives you more control over connectivity costs and coverage quality.

### Multi-Carrier Redundancy Expansion

**Multi-carrier redundancy** — which automatically fails over to a backup carrier if the primary connection drops — is now available in **over 30 countries**, including the UK, Canada, Australia, and EU nations. This is a critical reliability improvement for international deployments.

## Intercoms: Full Mobile Calling & Access

Verkada intercoms now support **full mobile calling and access control**. You can receive intercom calls, unlock doors, and view live footage directly from your phone. This means your team can respond to visitors and manage entry points from anywhere, not just from a desk.

## Sensors: Tougher Hardware & Better Mobile Experience

### Vandalism-Resistant Mount (ACC-SV-MOUNT-1)

A new **vandalism-resistant sensor mount** features a reinforced mounting plate that minimizes the risk of unauthorized removal. This is ideal for sensors deployed in public-facing or high-traffic areas where tampering is a concern.

### Redesigned Mobile Interface

The sensor experience on mobile gets a **redesigned interface** with enhanced data visualization and improved user actions. Monitoring environmental conditions on the go is now more intuitive and informative.

## Workplace: Flexible Guest Management

### Student Guest Type Flexibility

The **student guest type** now supports conditional logic matching the capabilities of standard guest types. This gives educational institutions more flexibility in how they manage student check-ins and visitor workflows.

### Security Screen Enhancements

The security screening interface has been updated with an **improved match-detection design** and a separate view for limited-information profiles. These changes help front desk staff and security teams process visitors more efficiently and accurately.
