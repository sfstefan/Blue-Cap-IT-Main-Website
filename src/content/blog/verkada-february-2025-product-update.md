Verkada's February 2025 product update brings a wide range of new features and improvements across the entire platform — from brand-new camera hardware and a game-changing search tool to smarter alarm capabilities and deeper access control integrations. Here is a comprehensive look at everything in this release.

## Cameras: New Hardware, Faster Search & Better Performance

### New CD43, CD43-E, CD53, and CD53-E Dome Cameras

Verkada has introduced four new **5MP dome cameras** — the CD43, CD43-E, CD53, and CD53-E. These models feature **enhanced low-light performance** and **advanced computer vision processors**, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor environments where clear image quality around the clock is essential.

### History Player Search

One of the standout additions this release is **History Player Search**, a powerful tool that enables **instantaneous footage discovery across multiple cameras**. Instead of manually scrubbing through hours of video, you can now construct chronological timelines that stitch together events across your camera network — dramatically speeding up investigations and incident reviews.

### Revamped People and Vehicle Pages

The search interfaces for People and Vehicles have been completely **reorganized with granular filtering capabilities**. These improvements make it faster and easier to identify specific individuals or vehicles across your footage, cutting down on the time it takes to find what you need.

### Person of Interest Only Face Search

A new **privacy-focused feature** limits facial recognition storage to pre-defined Person of Interest (POI) lists only. This gives organizations greater control over how facial data is stored and used, helping to balance security needs with privacy requirements.

### CF83-E-F Fisheye and CP63-E-F PTZ: FIPS 140-2 Validated

The **CF83-E-F Fisheye (12.5MP)** and **CP63-E-F PTZ (4K)** cameras now include **FIPS 140-2-validated encryption**, making them approved for government and regulated-industry deployments that require the highest levels of data security.

### Command Connector ONVIF Support

Verkada's Command Connector has been recognized as **ONVIF Profile S conformant**, and now goes a step further by also ingesting **non-conformant RTSP-compatible cameras**. This means you can bring virtually any existing camera into your Verkada Command platform, regardless of manufacturer.

### Performance Improvements

Verkada has made significant under-the-hood improvements, including **faster camera homepage and settings loading** and up to a **30% reduction in Time to First Frame** for historical playback. These enhancements mean less waiting and a smoother day-to-day experience for operators.

### CD62/CD62-E End-of-Sale Notice

The CD62 and CD62-E dome cameras have been **discontinued as of May 1, 2025**. Existing users are directed toward the newer CD63 models as replacements.

## Connectivity: New Outdoor Wi-Fi Gateway

### GW31-E Outdoor Wi-Fi Gateway

The new **GW31-E Outdoor Wi-Fi Gateway** simplifies camera deployment in challenging locations such as parking lots, fence lines, and other outdoor areas where running Ethernet is impractical. This gateway extends Wi-Fi connectivity to get your cameras online without complex cabling.

## Alarms: Wireless Expansion, Smarter Triggers & Badge-to-Disarm

### WH32 Repeater and WH52 Hub

Verkada is expanding wireless alarm coverage with the **WH32 Repeater** and **WH52 Hub**, designed to provide reliable alarm connectivity across sites of varying sizes — from small offices to large campuses.

### Badge-to-Disarm

One of the most convenient new features is **Badge-to-Disarm**, which allows employees to use their existing access credentials (badge, fob, or mobile pass) to disarm the alarm system. No more fumbling with codes at the door — just tap and go.

### Line-Crossing Triggers

**Line-Crossing Triggers** create digital perimeters that activate alarms when someone crosses a defined boundary in a specific direction. This is ideal for protecting restricted areas, loading docks, or fence lines without the need for physical barriers.

### Partition-Based Alarms

You can now configure **multiple independent alarm responses per site** based on which sensors are triggered. This means different areas of a building (e.g., a warehouse vs. an office floor) can have their own alarm behavior and response protocols.

### Partition-Based Keycodes

Building on partition support, **site administrators can now configure keycodes with selective partition access** — giving different team members control over only the areas relevant to their role.

### Improved Incident Link

The Incident Link feature now provides **real-time visibility of concurrent users** working on the same incident, plus a **direct police dispatch capability** for faster emergency response.

### External Response Contacts

**Non-Command users can now receive alarm notifications**, making it easy to loop in external security teams, building managers, or other stakeholders who do not have a full Verkada account.

## Access Control: Deeper Integrations & Offline Lockdowns

### SimonsVoss SmartIntego Integration

Verkada now integrates with **SimonsVoss SmartIntego** wireless locks, enabling **remote unlock functionality** and **live door event monitoring** directly from the Verkada Command platform.

### Third-Party OSDP Door Reader Support

**Expanded compatibility with third-party OSDP door readers** means more flexibility when designing or upgrading your access control setup — you are not locked into a single hardware ecosystem.

### Atrium Integration for Higher Education

The new **Atrium integration** automates **user and credential synchronization** specifically for higher education institutions, simplifying the management of student and staff access across campus.

### Offline Lockdowns with AUX Outputs

A critical safety feature: **lockdown enforcement now persists during connectivity loss**. Even if your network goes down, lockdown commands remain active. Additionally, AUX outputs now support triggering **strobes and sirens** during lockdown events for enhanced on-site alerting.

### Door Exceptions API

The new **Door Exceptions API** allows programmatic schedule modifications via third-party software — perfect for integrating access control with your existing building management or scheduling systems.

### Access Level API

The **Access Level API** enables dynamic updates to doors, schedules, and groups from external systems, giving IT teams the ability to automate access management at scale.

### Non-Unique MFA Codes

**Dedicated multi-factor authentication codes** can now be configured separately, adding an extra layer of security at sensitive entry points.

### Favorite Doors (iOS Pass App)

Users of the iOS Pass App can now **pin frequently accessed doors** to a favorites list for quick, one-tap access — a small but meaningful quality-of-life improvement for daily use.

### Roll Call Reports (General Release)

**Roll Call Reports** are now generally available and integrate with **Verkada Guest** to include visitors in headcounts — essential for emergency evacuations and compliance scenarios.

## Command Platform: Smarter Alerts, Better Collaboration & Mobile Upgrades

### Microsoft Teams and Slack Integrations

Verkada Command now integrates with **Microsoft Teams and Slack**, allowing your team to manage security alerts directly within the communication platforms they already use every day.

### Alert Inbox Enhancement

The **Alert Inbox** has been redesigned to expedite incident response with better event organization on both web and mobile — helping operators prioritize and act on alerts faster.

### Enriched Notifications

**Mobile notifications now include detailed event context**, so security personnel can review and respond to incidents directly from their phone without needing to open the full Command dashboard.

### User Directories and Licensing Groups (Beta)

A new beta feature introduces **centralized user management with local team autonomy** — ideal for organizations with multiple sites or departments that need both oversight and independence.

### Global Site Admin Role

**Org Admins can now grant themselves site-wide administrative access**, streamlining management for administrators who oversee multiple locations.

### Org Subdomain Login

**Consolidated login access** for users who hold accounts across multiple organizations within the same domain — reducing friction for managed service providers and multi-site operators.

### Command User Admin Role

A new **Command User Admin role** allows delegation of user management tasks without granting full organizational authority — great for distributing administrative workload securely.

### VPAT 2025 Compliance

Verkada has achieved **VPAT 2025 accessibility compliance** across the platform, ensuring the Command interface meets current accessibility standards.

### Vehicles Tab (Mobile App)

The mobile app now includes a **Vehicles tab** for viewing detected vehicles and license plates on the go — useful for parking management and vehicle-related investigations.

### Mobile Camera Setup

Setting up new cameras is now easier with **QR code scanning** for device commissioning and configuration directly from a mobile device.

### Mobile Alarms App

The **Mobile Alarms App** brings full alarm management to your phone, including site arming/disarming, incident response, and access to historical alarm activity.

## Intercoms: Emergency Configuration Mode

### Emergency Configuration Mode

Verkada intercoms now support a specialized **Emergency Configuration Mode** with enhanced settings for call-for-aid scenarios. This ensures that during emergencies, intercom behavior is optimized for rapid communication and response.

## Sensors: Custom Indices & Government-Grade Security

### Four Custom Index Readings

Verkada Air Quality Sensors now support **four custom index readings**: **Humidex**, **Dew Point**, **RESET Viral Index**, and **Mold Risk Index**. These go beyond standard temperature and humidity monitoring to give facility managers deeper insight into environmental conditions that affect occupant health and comfort.

### SV25-F FIPS-Validated Sensor

The new **SV25-F sensor** is a **FIPS-validated, government-grade option** with enhanced security layers — meeting the stringent requirements of federal and regulated environments.

### Enhanced Third-Party VMS Data

Sensor readings shared with third-party video management systems now include **name, value, threshold, and timestamp** — providing richer context for automated alerts and reporting.

### i-Pro Video Insight Integration

A new **pre-built VMS connection with i-Pro Video Insight** enables sensor event alerts with visual context, bridging the gap between environmental monitoring and video surveillance.

## Workplace: Streamlined Visitor Management

### On-Demand Access Credentials

**Visitor check-ins are now simpler** with dynamic credential generation, allowing front desk staff to issue temporary access on the fly without pre-registration.

### Multi-Day Events

**Verkada Guest now supports multi-day events** with a single registration spanning multiple event days — eliminating the need for visitors to re-register each day of a conference or multi-day visit.

### Advance Security Screening

A new **pre-registration workflow** allows guests to submit a government ID photo in advance, enabling security screening before they arrive on site.

### Improved Guest Events Page

The Guest Events page has been **enhanced with better visibility** for present, future, and past events — making it easier for administrators to manage visitor activity at a glance.
