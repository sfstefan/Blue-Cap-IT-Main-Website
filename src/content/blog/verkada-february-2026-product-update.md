Verkada's February 2026 product update is one of the most feature-packed releases yet, delivering powerful new capabilities across the entire platform. From AI-powered deterrence that stops intruders in their tracks to live translation on intercoms, here's everything you need to know.

## Cameras: Smarter Detection & Faster Response

### Compound Alerts

One of the biggest additions this release is **compound alerts**, which let you combine multiple alert conditions into a single trigger. For example, you can set an alert that only fires when both motion *and* specific attribute criteria are met — dramatically reducing false positives and making sure your team only gets notified when it truly matters. *(Available March 30, 2026)*

### LPR Zones for Parking Management

**License Plate Recognition (LPR) zones** now enable monitoring of vehicle occupancy, on-site presence, and dwell times across parking facilities. This is a game-changer for managing parking lots, garages, and vehicle-heavy environments. *(Available March 30, 2026)*

### Fighting & Loud Noise Detection

Verkada cameras can now detect **physical altercations** and send fighting detection notifications in real time. Combined with new **loud noise detection** via onboard microphones on select models, your team gets alerted to potential incidents faster than ever.

### AI-Powered Incident Summaries

When activity detection identifies someone falling, **automatic incident reports** are now generated. On top of that, **AI-powered incident summaries** can auto-generate descriptions for archived footage within an incident report with a single click — saving your team significant time during investigations.

### Improved Search & Archiving

The **unified timeline search** has been upgraded with a new model that combines facial analytics with appearance signals for more accurate results. You can now convert a unified timeline directly into an incident report — including archiving all associated footage — with one click.

### Smart Trends on Mobile

**Smart trends dashboards** are now accessible on the Command mobile app with configurable widgets, along with device stats dashboards for monitoring fleet health on the go. Historical footage loading times have also been improved on mobile.

## Alarms: AI-Powered Deterrence Goes Public Beta

### AI-Powered Deterrence

This is a standout feature: **AI-powered deterrence** is now in public beta. Organizations can stop unwanted activity with human-like voice warnings that respond intelligently to the situation. The system uses **random voice rotation** to prevent offenders from becoming desensitized to repeated warnings. Deterrence functionality now extends to intercoms as well, perfect for discreet entrance and gate installations.

### Keypad Upgrades

The **BK22 keypad** gets a major upgrade with live camera feeds for situational awareness, plus **two-way audio** that enables direct communication between your security operations center and on-site staff during emergencies. Keypad settings can now be managed remotely through Command — no physical access required.

### Sensor Event Alerts & Incident Integration

Alarm sensor events now cover **Door Held Open**, **Motion Detected**, and **Water Leak Detected** scenarios. Historical alarm events can be archived to incident management with one click.

## Access Control: Streamlined & Expanded

- **Pass App onboarding** has been streamlined for Android users with an improved first-time experience
- The **AF64 Access Station Pro** is now open for early access program requests
- The **Command mobile app** expands access control functionality, including key setting adjustments and roll call initiation

## Intercoms: Live Translation & AI Voice Directory

### Live Translation

Verkada intercoms now support **live translation** with two-way voice and text translation. This is a major enhancement for organizations with multilingual visitors or staff — communication barriers are effectively eliminated at the door.

### AI-Powered Voice Directory

The **TD63 intercom** now features an AI-powered voice directory that uses natural language processing to route calls without needing a receptionist. Visitors simply say who they're looking for, and the system handles the rest.

### New Hardware & Installation Options

- A **security relay accessory** protects door lock circuitry by keeping power components on the secure side
- **Reader over network** transmits credential scans via existing network infrastructure, eliminating the need for hardwired connections
- The **TD33 surface mount variant** addresses installation constraints in tight or crowded spaces

## Command Platform

- **Audit logs** now feature pre-filtered views for common scenarios with automated report scheduling
- **Site Planner collaborative mode** lets multiple stakeholders view and update plans simultaneously *(April release)*
- **Mobile app support** extends to FedRAMP In Process (Moderate) GovCloud environments
- **Trailers** are now accessible on mobile for remote power and usage monitoring
- **AI-powered voice search** on mobile lets you query footage using natural language

## Workplace

- **Biometric ID verification** compares driver's license images to check-in kiosk face captures
- **AF64 face unlock** enables visitor door access using facial recognition *(later in 2026)*
- New **APIs for approved lists and guest events** enable integration with third-party systems
- **Guest Pass QR codes** now work at intercoms for seamless exterior door access
- **Customizable badge designer** supports strict branding and compliance requirements
- **PowerSchool integration** exits beta for direct attendance data transfer

## Connectivity

- **MT81 Cloud-Managed Security Trailer** now supports AWS GovCloud
- **Remote power cycling** enables device troubleshooting without site visits
- **Low-power alerts** notify when trailer battery drops below preset thresholds
- **Geofence alerts** trigger when buses or trailers leave approved zones
