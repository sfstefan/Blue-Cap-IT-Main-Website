## Why Cybersecurity Matters More Than Ever for SMBs

Cyberattacks aren't just a big-business problem. In fact, small and mid-sized businesses are increasingly the primary targets. Why? Because most SMBs don't have dedicated security teams, their defenses are often outdated or incomplete, and attackers know it.

Ransomware, phishing, business email compromise, and data breaches can devastate a small business — leading to costly downtime, lost customer trust, and even regulatory penalties. The challenge has always been that the cybersecurity tools powerful enough to stop these threats were built for large enterprises with large budgets and dedicated IT security staff.

That's where Coro changes the game.

## What Is Coro?

Coro is an **all-in-one cybersecurity platform purpose-built for small and mid-sized businesses**. Instead of piecing together a patchwork of security tools from different vendors, Coro brings everything into a single platform with a single lightweight agent — giving you complete protection without the complexity.

Coro's AI-powered engine automatically detects, investigates, and resolves the vast majority of threats without requiring manual intervention. That means your business stays protected around the clock, even without a full-time security team.

## What Coro Protects

Coro's modular platform covers every major attack surface that SMBs face today:

- **Endpoint Protection** — Next-generation antivirus and EDR (Endpoint Detection & Response) for every laptop, desktop, and server. Automatically detects and contains malware, ransomware, and advanced threats.

- **Email Security** — Blocks phishing attempts, business email compromise, malicious attachments, and spam before they ever reach your inbox. Works seamlessly with Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace.

- **Cloud App Security** — Monitors and protects your cloud applications like Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Slack, and Salesforce. Detects unauthorized access, data leaks, and suspicious activity across your cloud environment.

- **Network Security** — Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA), VPN, firewall, and DNS filtering to control who can access your network and what they can reach — whether your team is in the office or working remotely.

- **Data Governance** — Discovers, classifies, and protects sensitive data across your organization. Helps enforce policies to prevent data loss and maintain compliance with industry regulations.

- **Security Awareness Training** — Engaging training programs and simulated phishing campaigns that educate your employees and turn your team from a potential vulnerability into your first line of defense.
