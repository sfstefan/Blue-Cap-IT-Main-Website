## What Huntress Protects

The Huntress platform covers the full spectrum of modern cyber threats through four integrated modules:

### Managed EDR (Endpoint Detection & Response)

Huntress Managed EDR goes far beyond traditional antivirus. A lightweight agent runs on every endpoint — Windows, Mac, and Linux — using behavioral analytics and machine learning to detect advanced threats like fileless malware, living-off-the-land attacks, and ransomware. When a threat is confirmed, the SOC contains and remediates it automatically. No alert fatigue. No false positive overload.

**Key stat:** Huntress has an industry-leading **8-minute mean time to respond (MTTR)** — meaning threats are stopped before attackers can move laterally through your network.

### Managed ITDR (Identity Threat Detection & Response)

Identity-based attacks are exploding. Attackers don't need to hack your firewall when they can just log in with stolen credentials. Huntress ITDR monitors your **Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace** environments for account takeovers, business email compromise, phishing, Adversary-in-the-Middle (AitM) attacks, and rogue OAuth apps — then stops them with a **3-minute mean time to respond**.

### Managed SIEM (Security Information & Event Management)

Most businesses need SIEM for compliance (HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, cyber insurance) but can't afford the complexity and cost of traditional solutions. Huntress Managed SIEM handles log ingestion, correlation, threat detection, and 24/7 monitoring — all fully managed by SOC experts at a predictable, transparent price. No surprise ingestion fees. No six-figure deployments.

### Security Awareness Training (SAT)

Your employees are your first line of defense — or your biggest vulnerability. Huntress SAT uses engaging content created by **Emmy Award-winning animators** and cybersecurity experts, hands-on tradecraft simulations that put employees in attackers' shoes, and realistic phishing tests based on current threat intelligence. It's training that actually changes behavior, not just checks a compliance box.
