Verkada's August 2024 product update delivers a sweeping set of improvements across the entire platform. Highlights include the ability to bring non-Verkada cameras into the Command platform, Apple Wallet integration for access control, smarter AI-powered video search, and a new fourth-generation door reader. Here is a full breakdown of everything in this release.

## Cameras: AI Search, Command Connector & Extended Cloud Backup

### Command Connector

One of the biggest announcements this cycle is **Command Connector**, which lets organizations integrate non-Verkada cameras directly into the Command platform. This means you can manage mixed-vendor camera environments from a single pane of glass — and those third-party cameras gain access to Verkada's AI-powered search capabilities, with future support planned for advanced features like **Person of Interest (POI)** and **License Plate of Interest (LPOI)** detection. If you have legacy cameras you are not ready to replace, Command Connector gives them new life.

### AI-Powered Search Updates

Verkada's AI-powered video search gets a significant upgrade with **boolean operators** — you can now use "NOT," "AND," and "OR" to build detailed, precise search queries during investigations. On top of that, a new **"More like this" button** lets you quickly find similar footage to a clip you have already identified, making it faster than ever to piece together a timeline of events.

### Extended Cloud Backup

Cloud backup has been simplified with a new structure based on **30-day increment packs**, making it easier to right-size your retention needs. A new **self-claim portal** also gives customers more control over managing their backup subscriptions without having to go through support.

### FIPS PTZ & Multisensor Cameras

For government and high-security customers, Verkada introduces two new **FIPS-compliant camera models**: the **CP52-E-F** (PTZ) and the **CH52-E-F** (Multisensor). These models meet the enhanced cryptographic standards required by federal agencies and other regulated environments.

### Camera Audio API

A new **Camera Audio API** enables integration between Verkada cameras and external audio systems. This opens up possibilities for synchronized announcements, alert-triggered audio responses, and other workflows that bridge video and audio infrastructure.

## Access Control: New Door Reader, Apple Wallet & Cross-Site Lockdowns

### AD34 Door Reader

Verkada's **fourth-generation AD34 Door Reader** arrives with **wide-ranging credential support**, improved scan performance, and enhanced **Bluetooth unlock protection**. It is a meaningful step up in both speed and security compared to previous models.

### Apple Wallet Integration

Employees can now unlock doors using **encrypted NFC badges stored in Apple Wallet** on their iPhone or Apple Watch. This eliminates the need to carry a separate physical badge — your phone or watch becomes your credential, with full encryption protecting every transaction.

### Credential Auto-Deactivate

A new **credential auto-deactivate** feature automatically disables physical credentials that have been inactive for a set period. When someone tries to use a deactivated credential, administrators are notified immediately — adding an important layer of security for managing badge lifecycles across large organizations.

### Cross-Site Lockdowns

In an emergency, security teams can now trigger **cross-site lockdowns** that protect multiple nearby buildings simultaneously. This is especially valuable for campus environments, multi-building offices, or school districts where a threat in one location demands an immediate response across the surrounding area.

## Command: Australia Data Center

Verkada expands its global infrastructure with a new **Australia data center**, providing local processing and storage for customers in the Australian region. This means faster performance and helps organizations meet local data residency requirements.

## Connectivity: Power Monitoring, VPN Support & More

### Device Power Usage

Administrators can now track **live and historical power consumption** for devices connected through cellular gateways. This visibility helps with capacity planning, troubleshooting, and identifying devices that may be drawing more power than expected.

### Disconnected Device History

A new **30-day history of recently connected devices** makes it easier to diagnose connectivity issues. The feature also includes **PoE port cycling** capability, letting you remotely restart devices without a physical visit to the network closet.

### LTE Data via PoE In Port

The **GC31-E cellular gateway** now supports a configuration option to receive LTE data through its PoE In port, reducing installation complexity by working with existing PoE switches — no separate power or data connections required.

### VPN Support

Verkada's cellular gateways gain **IPsec VPN client functionality**, enabling secure integration with RTSP video streams and Fusus platforms. This is an important capability for organizations that need to extend Verkada connectivity through encrypted tunnels.

## Intercoms: Dashboards, AI Transcription Search & Enhanced Controls

### Intercom Dashboard

A new **fleet health and usage dashboard** gives administrators visual metrics on their intercom deployment — making it easy to spot trends, identify underperforming devices, and understand how intercoms are being used across your sites.

### Events Reporting

Intercom administrators can now **export call data to CSV**, covering calls placed, admits granted, and entries refused. This is a major quality-of-life improvement for organizations that need to audit visitor interactions or generate compliance reports.

### Call Flow Controls

Enhanced **call flow controls** give administrators finer-grained management over how intercom calls are routed. New capabilities include improved receiver type designation, **view-only and edit access provisioning**, and streamlined link management.

### Intercom Alerts

New **intercom alert types** cover a range of events including calls triggered, missed calls, door unlocks, and SIP errors. These notifications ensure your team stays informed about intercom activity without having to monitor dashboards manually.

### AI-Powered Transcription Search

You can now search intercom call recordings using **natural language text queries**. This AI-powered transcription search lets you quickly find call events where specific terms or topics were mentioned — invaluable for investigations and compliance reviews.

## Sensors: Video Overlay & Noise Calibration

### Sensor Graph and Camera Overlay

Verkada introduces a **consolidated video feed with overlaid sensor data**, letting you view environmental readings (temperature, humidity, air quality, and more) directly on top of the corresponding camera footage. This gives you instant visual context for any sensor alert.

### Noise Reading Calibration

A new **self-calibration feature** for noise decibel sensors ensures your readings are accurate to the specific environment where the sensor is installed. This eliminates guesswork and improves the reliability of noise-based alerts and reporting.

## Workplace: Admin Permissions & Subsite Support

### Command Admin Permissions

User permissions can now be managed directly from the **Users, Sites, and Groups pages** within Command — no need to navigate to a separate admin console. This streamlines day-to-day user management for organizations of all sizes.

### Subsite Support

**Verkada Guest and Mailroom** now fully support subsites, meaning you can deploy visitor management and package tracking at specific facility locations within a larger site. This is ideal for multi-building campuses or organizations with distinct reception areas.
