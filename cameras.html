Why Verkada Cameras

Smarter Cameras.
Zero Complexity.

Verkada cameras combine on-board AI processing with cloud management to deliver enterprise-grade surveillance without the enterprise-grade headaches.

🧠

On-Board AI Analytics

Each Verkada camera processes AI analytics locally — no separate servers required. Get real-time intelligence built into every device.

  • Person & vehicle detection with instant alerts
  • License plate recognition (LPR) on compatible models
  • Facial recognition & watchlist alerts
  • Crowd detection and occupancy monitoring
  • Motion search — find footage in seconds
☁️

True Cloud Management

Every camera is managed through Verkada Command — a browser-based dashboard accessible from anywhere. No VPN, no NVR, no on-premise server.

  • Live view and playback from any browser or mobile app
  • Automatic firmware updates pushed by Verkada
  • Multi-site management from one unified dashboard
  • Granular user permissions and audit logs
  • 10-year hardware warranty on all cameras
💾

Hybrid On-Board Storage

Video footage is stored directly on the camera's built-in solid-state drive — up to 365 days of local storage — and backed up to the cloud simultaneously.

  • No NVR or DVR required — ever
  • Up to 365 days of on-board video retention
  • Automatic cloud backup included
  • Footage survives network outages
  • Tamper-evident encrypted storage
