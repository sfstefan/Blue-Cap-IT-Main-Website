🌡️

Temperature & Humidity

Protect server rooms, storage areas, and sensitive equipment with continuous monitoring and instant threshold alerts. 90-day historical data included.

💨

Air Quality (IAQ)

Monitor CO2, VOC, PM2.5 particulates, and humidity levels. Ideal for offices, schools, and healthcare environments focused on occupant health.

💧

Water Leak Detection

Detect water intrusion before it causes damage. Place sensors under HVAC units, near pipes, and in server rooms for early warning alerts.

🏃

Motion Detection

Passive infrared (PIR) motion sensors that integrate directly with your camera system and access control — perfect for after-hours security triggers.

🚪

Door & Window Contacts

Know instantly when any door or window is opened or left ajar. Works in tandem with alarms and access control for complete perimeter awareness.

Unified Dashboard

All sensor data lives in Verkada Command alongside your cameras and access control. One login, one view, complete situational awareness.
