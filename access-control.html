Cloud-First Door Management

Verkada's access control is managed entirely in the cloud through Verkada Command. Add users, assign access levels, and unlock doors from your phone — no on-site server needed.

  • Remote unlock from any device, anywhere
  • Instant credential provisioning & revocation
  • Scheduled access rules & time-based permissions
  • Full audit trail of every door event
  • Unified with cameras for visual verification

Modern Credential Options

Offer employees and visitors the credential experience that works for them — from traditional keycards to smartphone-based mobile access, Apple Watch, and PIN codes.

  • RFID card & fob credentials
  • Mobile credentials via Verkada Pass app
  • Apple Watch & wearable support
  • PIN code entry & QR code visitor access
  • Biometric reader support
🔗

Seamless Camera Integration

Every access event is linked to a camera clip — instantly see who badged through any door, on any day, in seconds. True unified physical security.

  • Auto-linked video clip for every door event
  • One-click jump from access log to camera footage
  • Tailgating & forced door alerts
  • Door-held-open notifications
  • Integrated with alarms and sensors
